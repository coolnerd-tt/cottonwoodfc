Common Questions

Fee FAQ

When do I pay fees?

Club fees are typically due at registration. Your Team Manager will communicate the exact payment schedule and any available payment plan options.

Is there a fee to try out?

No — tryouts are completely free. You only pay fees after your player is placed on a team and you register via UYSA SportsAffinity.

What is the UYSA registration fee?

UYSA charges an annual player registration fee (typically $30–$50 depending on age). This goes directly to UYSA, not Cottonwood FC.

Are there financial assistance options?

UYSA offers a scholarship program for families who qualify. Contact us at info@cottonwoodfc.org for more information.

What does the club fee cover?

Club fees cover certified coaching staff, field permits at Butler Fields, training equipment, and team management support.

Can I get a refund if my player doesn't make a team?

If your player is not placed on a team, no fees are due. Registration fees are only collected after placement and team assignment.

Our Purpose

The mission of Cottonwood Football Club is to develop young men and women of character and integrity by cultivating their growth as soccer players and a life-long passion for the 'beautiful game.'

⚡

Build Players

Principle-based coaching that develops soccer IQ and technical skills without position locks — everyone plays, everyone develops.

❤️

Build People

We develop character and integrity alongside athletic ability. Respectful coaches who players want to emulate.

🏆

Build Champions

73 teams competing at the highest levels across UYSA Premier, SCL, IRL, and X-League. Real competition. Real results.

How We Coach

Player Development
Done Right

At Cottonwood FC, we believe in building complete players — not just athletes who follow instructions, but thinkers who understand the game and love to play it.

Meet Our Coaches →
🎯
Everyone Plays

No position lock until older ages. Every player gets significant time on the field — we develop the whole player.

🧠
Think, Don't Just Perform

We avoid "joystick" coaching. Players learn to read the game and make decisions — we guide, not dictate.

⚽
Play More, Wait Less

Training prioritizes playing time — minimal lines, laps, and lectures. Maximum touches on the ball.

💪
Build Players & People

Coaches correct players without being demeaning. We build character and integrity alongside athletic skill.

What We Ask of You
Our Expectations of Parents
📣

Cheer & Encourage

Be the loudest voice in your player's corner. Cheer, encourage, and support — and let the coaches do the coaching on the sideline.

🤝

Positive Game Conduct

Only positive commentary toward referees and opposing players. We compete hard and we compete clean — that's the Cottonwood FC standard.

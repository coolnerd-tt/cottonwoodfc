What to Expect

How Tryouts Work

01

Find Your Age Group

Locate your player's age group in the schedule below. Check in 30 minutes before your session begins.

02

Show Up at Butler Fields

West of Butler Middle School on 7800 South. Bring cleats, shinguards, and a water bottle. No fee at the door.

03

Register Online

After tryouts, register via UYSA SportsAffinity. You'll need an assignment code from your Team Manager.

04

Wait for Placement

Coaches evaluate all players and assign teams. Players are placed based on skill level and team needs.

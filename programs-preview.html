Academy
U6 – U8 · Ages 5–8

Academy Program

Our youngest players discover the beautiful game in a fun, low-pressure, skill-building environment. No competitive league play — just love of the game, fundamentals, and teammates.

  • ✅ Ages 5–8 (U6 & U8 divisions)
  • ✅ Non-competitive, recreational format
  • ✅ Small-sided games (3v3 to 5v5)
  • ✅ Focus on fun, fundamentals, and friendship
  • ✅ No tryouts required for Academy
Learn More →
Competitive
U10 – U12 · Ages 9–12

Competitive Program

Structured competition through UYSA, SCL, and IRL leagues. Players develop positional identity, tactical awareness, and consistent training habits in a supportive team environment.

  • ✅ U10 and U12 divisions
  • ✅ UYSA, SCL, and IRL league play
  • ✅ Multiple practices per week
  • ✅ Certified coaching staff
  • ✅ Tryouts required — free to attend
View Tryout Dates →
Premier
U14 – U17 · Ages 13–17

Premier Program

High-level competitive soccer for serious players. UYSA Premier and SCL competition with intensive training, tournament play, and college-preparation exposure for older players.

  • ✅ U14 through U17 divisions
  • ✅ UYSA Premier & SCL competition
  • ✅ Tournament and showcase events
  • ✅ College recruitment support (U16+)
  • ✅ Tryouts required — free to attend
View Tryout Dates →
Elite
U18 – U19 · Ages 18–19

Elite Program

Top-flight competitive soccer for our most advanced players. Full commitment required. Focused preparation for college programs, club teams, and competitive post-high school opportunities.

  • ✅ U18 and U19 divisions
  • ✅ UYSA Premier competition
  • ✅ College exposure events
  • ✅ High-performance training environment
  • ✅ Tryouts required — free to attend

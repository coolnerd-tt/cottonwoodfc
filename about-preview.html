Our Story

How We Got Here

Cottonwood FC was founded in 2014 in Cottonwood Heights, Utah, with a simple idea: build a soccer club that develops the whole player — not just athletes, but people of character and integrity.

From a small group of founding families, CFC has grown to become one of Utah's largest and most respected youth soccer clubs — 73 teams, 1,261 players, and counting.

We're proud of our annual Butlerville Days Parade participation and our deep roots in the Cottonwood Heights community — a tradition since our founding year.

How We Coach

Our Development Philosophy

At Cottonwood FC, we believe in building complete players — not just athletes who follow instructions, but thinkers who understand the game and love to play it.

Our coaches are trained to guide, not dictate. We focus on developing soccer intelligence, decision-making, and a genuine love for the beautiful game.

What We Ask of You
Our Expectations of Parents
📣

Cheer & Encourage

Be the loudest voice in your player's corner. Cheer, encourage, and support — let the coaches do the coaching.

🤝

Positive Game Conduct

Only positive commentary toward referees and opposing players. We compete hard and clean — that's the CFC standard.

🙌

Trust the Process

Development takes time. Trust that our coaches have a plan for every player's growth.

📱

Communicate Respectfully

Questions? Reach out to your Team Manager or coach directly — not through the sideline during a match.
